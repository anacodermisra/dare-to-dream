About Dare to Dream

"Dare to Dream" is the flagship ideathon by the Ministry of Defence and DRDO, aiming to identify and nurture innovative minds across the country. This national-level challenge invites participation from individuals, startups, and MSMEs to solve real-world problems in critical sectors like AI/ML, Aerospace, Life Sciences, VLSI, and Missile Technology.

The goal is to accelerate India's self-reliance in defence technologies by fostering indigenous innovations, offering mentorship, incubation, and opportunities for implementation. Whether you're a student, technologist, or entrepreneur — your ideas can shape the future of India's defence innovation ecosystem.

Innovation Verticals

AI / ML

Develop intelligent systems, automation, and decision-making tools to modernize defence strategy.

Aerospace

Innovate in flight systems, satellite tech, drones, and aircraft design for strategic advantage.

VLSI

Create cutting-edge chips and processors that power secure and efficient defence electronics.

Life Sciences

Work on biotechnology, health, nutrition, and environment for improving soldier well-being.

Missile Tech

Enhance missile systems, propulsion, and targeting technologies to defend the nation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any registration fee?

No, it’s completely free.

Can teams participate?

Yes, team submissions are allowed.

Who owns the IP?

You retain ownership; DRDO may request rights for implementation.

Can I submit multiple ideas?

Yes, but only one can be shortlisted.
